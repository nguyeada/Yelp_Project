Welcome to the Information Page!

Purpose: To provide the latitude and longitude coordinates of your current location then prompt the user to enter their desired cuisine and location then return a list of 16 restaurants fulfilling the request
Step 1: Allow the application to determine your specific location coordinates
Step 2: Look at the map provided on the bottom, does it match your current location
Step 3: Enter the desired cuisine or food
Step 4: Enter the desired location or zip code
Step 5: Press serach, and a table with the restaurant name and their respective Yelp website is provided
Step 6: Click on the chosen restaurant to read its reviews, pictures, and information
